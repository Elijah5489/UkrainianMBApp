Cultural Highlights

Traditional Music

Rich folk music traditions including bandura, kobza, and choral singing that continue to thrive in Manitoba's Ukrainian community.

Folk Dance

Colorful traditional dances like the Hopak and Kolomyika, performed at cultural events and festivals throughout the year.

Architecture

Distinctive church architecture and traditional building styles that mark Ukrainian settlements across Manitoba.
